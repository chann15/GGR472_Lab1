Spadina Station

Spadina Station has it's main enterence along Spadina and Bloor. It is also the only one of the 4 TTC stops that can access line 1 and 2.

St. George Station

St. George Station has it's 2 enterences along Bloor and St George and Bloor and Bedford. St. George Station is also the only other station that is on line 2.

Museum Station

Museum Station has 2 enterence's along either side of Queens Park Road, and another one located in Queens Park.

Queens Park Station

Queens Park Station has 2 enterence's along College Station on either side of University Avenue. It also has another enternece in the MaRS Discovery building.
